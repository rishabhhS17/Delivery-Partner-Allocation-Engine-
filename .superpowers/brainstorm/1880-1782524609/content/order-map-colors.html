Order Map — Route Line Colors

With Swiggy colors on riders (white/orange/deep-orange), how should the route lines look?

A

Match Rider State (Recommended)

Route line color matches the rider's current status dot — the line "belongs" to the rider visually. Leg 1 line = light orange, Leg 2 line = deep orange.

ACCEPTED — leg 1 (rider → restaurant)
PICKED_UP — leg 2 (restaurant → customer)
B

Neutral White

Single route color regardless of leg — semi-transparent white. Clean and never competes with the rider/restaurant/customer dots.

C

Destination Color

Line color matches where the rider is going: leg 1 ends at a restaurant (green line), leg 2 ends at a customer (cyan line). The line "points toward" its destination.

Leg 1 → Restaurant
Leg 2 → Customer
All options keep the same pin colors:  🟢 Restaurant    🔵 Customer drop    ⚫ Pending order (gray dot at restaurant)
